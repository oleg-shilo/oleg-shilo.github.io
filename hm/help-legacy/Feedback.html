Feedback and support

You can visit CS-Script home page (http://www.csscript.net) to check for updates, samples, on-line help or to submit your comments or bug reports.

You can check for available update by executing update.cs script:    

csws update

Send your bug reports to:
csscript.support@gmail.com (bug report)
or alternatively execute the following command in command-prompt:   

csws bug

Send you general feedback (comments, features for "wish list") to:
csscript.support@gmail.com (feedback)
or alternatively execute the following command in command-prompt:  

csws feedback
